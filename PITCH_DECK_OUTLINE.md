---

## 2. THE PROBLEM
### Slide: "The Crisis"
- **Context:** Thailand has 80,000+ youth at risk of entering informal labor (no contracts, exploitation, trafficking).
- **Pain Points:**
  - No accessible tools to model life decisions before committing.
  - Young people rely on gut feeling or peer pressure.
  - Legal labor protections are poorly understood.
  - Lack of awareness of safe, formal alternatives (vocational training, royal projects, scholarships).
  - High stress and instability when forced to choose too quickly.

### Data/Visual:
- Map showing at-risk youth by region
- Statistics on informal labor exploitation
- Income volatility visualizations

## 3. THE OPPORTUNITY
### Slide: "Why Now?"
- **Market Size:** 80,000+ youth annually in Thailand; regional expansion potential across SE Asia.
- **Sector Growth:** EdTech + FinTech + Social Impact (growing investor interest).
- **Government Alignment:** Thailand's 20-Year National Strategy supports vocational training, STEM education, and the Sufficiency Economy Philosophy (Royal Initiatives).
- **Timing:** COVID recovery + youth unemployment rebound + digital adoption surge.

---

## 4. THE SOLUTION
### Slide: "LifeSoft AI Platform"

**Three Core Modules:**

#### A. Life Simulator (Free)
- Profile → Decision Tree → Results workflow
- Users drag-and-drop life events (education, jobs, relocation, informal work).
- Real-time scoring (Opportunity Score, Stability Index).
- 10-year financial projections with stress testing.
- Legal guardian contract safety checker.

#### B. Opportunities Hub (Premium)
- Resume gap analysis
- Province opportunity map
- Smart path matcher (universities + internships)
- Deep internship matching
- CV roast & AI rewrites

#### C. Safe Pathways Engine (Free + Premium)
- Royal Path recommendations (5 vetted low-risk careers).
- Micro-scholarship matcher.
- Legal contract protection guides.
- Regional demand heatmaps.

### Key Differentiators:
- ✅ AI-driven, client-side processing (privacy-first).
- ✅ Royal Initiative integration (government collaboration).
- ✅ Legal protection built-in.
- ✅ Stress-test mode for scenario planning.
- ✅ Hyper-local (Thailand data + international scalability).

## 5. PRODUCT DEMO
### Slide: "See It In Action"
- **Flow Demo:**
  1. User creates profile (age, education, income, location).
  2. Simulator calculates Opportunity & Stability scores.
  3. Legal Guardian flags high-risk contract scenarios.
  4. User builds a decision tree (degree + job + relocation).
  5. AI recommends Royal Path alternatives.
  6. Results show 10-year projection with best-case/stress-test toggles.
