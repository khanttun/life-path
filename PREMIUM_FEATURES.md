# Premium Features — LifeSoft AI

This document outlines the premium tools and insights available to **Pro subscribers**. These features extend the free simulator with career‑building analytics, AI coaching, and deep opportunity matching designed for at‑risk youth navigating education and early careers.

---

## 🔒 Access
All premium features are visually locked across the application. Users can tap the lock icon anywhere in the navbar or open the **Premium Insights** panel to preview the tools and start a 30‑day free trial. A subscription (299 THB/month after trial) unlocks the full suite.

---

## 🧠 Tools Overview

### Resume Gap Analysis
- Upload your CV/portfolio to compare against ideal candidate profiles for internships and degree programs.
- Identifies missing skills, GPA shortfalls, and experience gaps.
- Suggests priority learning objectives.

### Province Opportunity Map
- Interactive map of Thailand showing regional demand, wages, and survival costs for different careers.
- Helps users choose where to study, intern, or start a business based on real‑time data.

### Smart Path Matcher
- Matches your profile (skills, interests, GPA, preferred province) to top universities and internship postings.
- Provides fit scores, estimated incomes, and survival scores.

### CV Roast & Refine
- AI evaluates keywords, ATS compatibility, and impact of bullet points.
- Offers rewritten examples that improve clarity and measurable outcomes.
- Includes metrics like keyword optimization and ATS friendliness.

### Deep Internship Matcher
- Uses radar charts to compare you vs. ideal candidates across competencies.
- Returns ranked internship opportunities with cultural‑fit, mobility, risk levels, and growth projections.

### Mock Interview Simulator
- Presents realistic, role‑specific interview questions generated by AI.
- Provides instant feedback on answers and guidance for improvement.

### Shadow Application Builder
- Generates professional cover letters and application drafts from templates.
- Can tailor content to specific job/scholarship listings using AI prompts.
